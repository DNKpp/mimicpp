Detailed Description

Contains reports of mimic++ types.

Reports are simplified object representations of mimic++ types. In fact, reports are used to communicate with independent domains (e.g. unit-test frameworks) over the IReporter interface and are thus designed to provide as much transparent information as possible, without requiring them to be a generic type.
